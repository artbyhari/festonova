Who We Are

We are Festonova India

Established in 2023, Festonova emerged with a fresh perspective on Event Management. Based in Mumbai, we combine the precision of project management with the soul of theatre.

We don't believe in "cookie-cutter" events. Every brand has a unique DNA, and our job is to express that DNA through physical space, sound, and light. From the chaotic energy of a hackathon to the refined silence of an art auction, we adapt our execution style to fit the narrative.

Frequently Asked Questions

What regions do you serve?

While we are headquartered in Mumbai, we actively manage events in Pune, Bangalore, Delhi NCR, and Hyderabad.

Do you handle small private parties?

We specialize in corporate events and large-scale productions. For private parties, we typically require a minimum budget threshold. Contact us for details.

Can you manage hybrid events?

Yes. Since 2023, 40% of our events have had a hybrid component. We manage the live stream, virtual platform, and physical audience simultaneously.

How much lead time do you need?

For large hackathons or launches, we recommend 2-3 months. However, our rapid response team can execute smaller events with 3 weeks notice.
